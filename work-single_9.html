The illustrations featured in this project were inspired by the events that occurred between 2020-2022. Each illustration was carefully crafted using a 3D software called Blender, which allowed for a high level of detail and realism. The illustrations showcase various aspects of our lives that were impacted by the global pandemic, such as remote work, social distancing, and virtual communication. The attention to detail and use of 3D modeling techniques bring these illustrations to life, making them a powerful representation of the events that shaped our world over the past few years. The illustrations serve as a reminder of the challenges we faced as a society and our ability to adapt and overcome them.
